About Hotel Elite Park in Tirupati

Hotel Elite Park is a mid-range hotel located in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, India. Situated near the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, it offers comfortable accommodation for pilgrims and tourists, with easy access to the sacred site.

Amenities

  • Well-furnished rooms with modern amenities
  • 24-hour front desk and room service
  • Free Wi-Fi throughout the property
  • Multi-cuisine restaurant and cafe
  • Parking facilities and travel desk
